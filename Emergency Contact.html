Tips for Better Emergency Support

  • Update Regularly: Keep your emergency contacts up to date to ensure quick access during emergencies.
  • Include Key Contacts: Add not just family members but also neighbors or friends who can help in a crisis.
  • Specify Contact Details: Clearly state the relationship and contact details for each person to avoid confusion.
  • Set Up Speed Dial: Program emergency numbers into your phone’s speed dial for rapid access.
  • Share Information: Inform trusted contacts about their role in emergencies, including how they should respond.
  • Back Up Contacts: Store important contacts in a cloud service or a printed copy in a safe location.
  • Test Your List: Regularly test the contacts by making sure their numbers are correct and they’re reachable.
