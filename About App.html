About Our App

Our Women Safety App is designed with your safety in mind. It provides a comprehensive suite of features to ensure you stay protected and connected in emergency situations. Below are some of the key functionalities:

Offline Location Sharing

In case of an emergency, the app allows you to share your location even if you are offline. This feature ensures that your location is transmitted to your emergency contacts or authorities when you need it the most.

Emergency Calls with Nearby Services

Our app can make emergency calls to police or pre-set emergency contacts using nearby Wi-Fi and Bluetooth services. This feature ensures that you can get help quickly, even if your phoneâ€™s network is unavailable.

Surrounding Scan

The app has the capability to scan the number of men and women around you, helping you assess your environment and identify potential threats. This feature enhances your situational awareness.

Audio Recording

With your permission, the app can record audio of your surroundings even when the phone is off. This feature ensures that important audio evidence is captured during critical moments.

Emergency Details Sharing

The app can share detailed information with your emergency contacts and the nearest police station, including your location, images, and statistics about people around you. This comprehensive approach ensures that help arrives promptly and effectively.
